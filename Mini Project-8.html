SIMS Hospital (SRM Institute for Medical Sciences) is a leading multi-specialty tertiary care hospital located in Chennai. It is part of the SRM Group and is recognized for providing advanced medical care across a wide range of specialties, including cardiac sciences, neurosciences, oncology, orthopaedics, gastroenterology, organ transplantation, and critical care.

The hospital offers comprehensive 360-degree healthcare services, combining experienced medical professionals, modern technology, and a patient-centered approach. SIMS has developed specialized institutes for areas such as cardiac sciences, neuroscience, orthopaedics, renal sciences, liver transplantation, oncology, and women's health, enabling multidisciplinary treatment for complex medical conditions.

Located in the Vadapalani area of Chennai, the hospital spans approximately 230,000 square feet and features advanced diagnostic facilities, specialized intensive care units, modern operation theatres, and multi-organ transplant programs. It serves both domestic and international patients through dedicated support services and personalized care

SIMS Hospital's mission is to deliver world-class healthcare by integrating clinical excellence, innovative technology, research, and compassionate patient care. The institution is widely regarded as one of South India's prominent centers for advanced tertiary and quaternary healthcare services.
