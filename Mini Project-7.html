About college

Emerald’s Degree Collegeis a private co-educational institution that was established in the year 2000. It is located in the Chittoor–Tirupati region of Andhra Pradesh, India, and was founded with the aim of providing accessible higher education to students in and around the Rayalaseema area. The college primarily focuses on undergraduate education and caters to students who wish to pursue degree courses close to their hometowns.

The college is affiliated with Sri Venkateswara University (SVU), Tirupati. Through this affiliation, the academic curriculum, examinations, and degree certifications are governed by the university standards. The institution offers undergraduate programs in streams such as Arts, Commerce, Science, and Management, including courses like B.Sc, B.Com, and BBA. Over time, it has also aligned some of its programs to meet job-oriented and skill-based education needs.

Since its establishment, Emerald’s Degree College has gradually developed its infrastructure and academic offerings. It has expanded its departments, improved classroom and basic facilities, and focused on providing affordable higher education to students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds. The college continues to function as a regular degree institution under university guidelines, serving as an option for students seeking general undergraduate education in the region.

The vision of Emerald’s Degree College is to provide quality education to young students so they can become among the best minds in the world. The college focuses on developing students with strong discipline, dedication, and determination, and aims to produce graduates who are well-prepared for modern competitive careers while also being responsible and value-driven individuals.

    In simple terms, their vision is to:
  • Deliver quality higher education
  • Build skilled and employable graduates
  • Promote discipline and moral values
  • Prepare students for global opportunities and challenges
