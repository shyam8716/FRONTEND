Emerald's Degree College Annual Fest 2026

Date:15–17 March 2026
Venue:Emerald's Degree College Campus

Emerald's Degree College Annual Fest 2026 is a vibrant celebration that brings together students, faculty, alumni, and guests for an exciting blend of academics, culture, sports, innovation, and entertainment. The event provides a platform for students to showcase their talents, develop new skills, build meaningful connections, and create lasting memories while celebrating the spirit of learning and teamwork.

Event objectives

  • Encourage students to showcase their talents and creativity.
  • Promote teamwork, leadership, and communication skills.
  • Foster innovation through academic and technical competitions.
  • Strengthen the sense of community within the college.
  • Provide opportunities for learning, networking, and personal growth.

Why Attend?

  • Participate in exciting cultural, technical, and sports events.
  • Learn from expert speakers and interactive workshops.
  • Showcase your skills and compete for exciting prizes.
  • Meet students, faculty, alumni, and industry professionals.
  • Create unforgettable memories with friends and the college community.
Key highlights
  • Cultural Performances
  • Technical Competitions & Hackathons
  • Sports Tournaments
  • Guest Speaker Sessions
  • Live Music & Entertainment
  • Art & Creative Exhibitions
  • Food Stalls & Fun Activities
  • Awards, Certificates, and Exciting Prizes
