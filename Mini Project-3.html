Day-1 Experience in Shirdi

First, we booked train tickets from Tirupati to Guntakal. It was a very enjoyable journey. After reaching Guntakal, our second train was delayed for a long time. When we finally reached Shirdi, we checked into a hotel, freshened up, and then went for Sai Baba Darshan. It took around 9 hours to complete the darshan, and by then it was already night.

Day-2 Experience in Shirdi

On the second day, we visited Sai Teerth. We watched a magic show, tried gun shooting, and enjoyed several adventure rides with our family. It was a fun and memorable experience.

Sai Teerth is a spiritual theme park in Shirdi that combines devotion, entertainment, and modern technology. It offers visitors unique attractions, including 5D shows, cultural exhibits, and experiences based on the life and teachings of Sai Baba.

The park provides an enjoyable experience for visitors of all ages. It blends spirituality with entertainment and helps devotees learn more about Sai Baba's message of faith, patience, love, and service.
