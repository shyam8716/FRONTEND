Dhee Coding Lab (DCL),founded in 2024, is a technology training and upskilling institute based in Bengaluru, India. It specializes in industry-oriented programs such as Full Stack Development, Data Science, Generative AI, Cloud Computing, and DevOps. Through hands-on projects, bootcamp-style learning, mentorship from experienced industry professionals, and placement support, Dhee Coding Lab aims to bridge the gap between academic education and real-world industry requirements, helping students become job-ready technology professionals.

Dhee Coding Lab (often abbreviated as DCL) is an India-based technology training and upskilling organization focused on preparing students and early-career professionals for software engineering, data science, AI, and related technology careers. According to the company, it operates as the professional education arm of ProDhee Technologies Private Limited.


Dhee Coding Lab presents its mission as helping create a "Skilled India" by providing practical, industry-oriented technology education. Its stated goals include

  • Improving employability among engineering and technology students.
  • Bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.
  • Providing hands-on project experience rather than purely theoretical instruction.
  • Expanding access to technology skills across different regions and socioeconomic groups.

Dhee Coding Lab currently operates multiple training centers in Bengaluru, including locations in:

  • Yelahanka
  • BTM Layout
  • Rajajinagar
It also offers online training programs for learners across India.
