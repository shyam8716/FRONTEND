About Us

Dhee Coding Labs is a tech training and upskilling institute that focuses on helping students become job-ready software developers and data professionals through structured, industry-oriented programs.

It offers intensive bootcamp-style courses in areas like full-stack development, data science, AI/ML, and cloud technologies. The training is designed to be highly practical, combining theory with hands-on projects, real-world simulations, and coding assignments so that learners gain actual development experience rather than just classroom knowledge.

The institute is known for its “career transformation” approach, where students are guided from fundamentals of programming to advanced topics like backend systems, machine learning, and deployment. Many of its programs also include mentorship from experienced industry professionals and exposure to product development workflows.

Overall, Dhee Coding Labs aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by focusing on skill development, project experience, and placement-oriented preparation.

Dhee Coding Lab was founded in 2024 according to its official company profile on LinkedIn

Dhee Coding Labs is generally listed as being based in India, with most public profiles and professional listings associating it with Hyderabad, Telangana, India as its operational headquarters.

However, like many training institutes, it may also operate across multiple locations or run programs in different cities through offline/online cohorts, so the exact “single HQ office” is not always consistently published across sources.

Dhee Coding Lab has roughly ~50 to 200 core employees, but may scale up to a few hundred when including trainers, interns, and partner staff.

For training-related roles and interns, the schedule is usually structured with fixed working hours, planned classes, and guided activities, which makes it more organized than typical corporate environments. However, during placement preparation periods or project deadlines, the workload can increase due to mock interviews, coding assessments, and target-driven tasks, which may create some pressure. Overall, it is not typically an extreme overtime or high-burnout environment, but it is also not very flexible or relaxed. Many people describe it as a bootcamp-style setup where learning and placement focus take priority, so the balance between work and personal time depends largely on how close the batch is to completion and job placement stages.

Dhee Coding Lab provides structured, job-oriented training programs designed to make students industry-ready through a bootcamp-style learning model. The training is typically 6 months long and focuses on building both technical skills and placement readiness.

The main training opportunities include full-stack development programs such as Java Full Stack with Cloud & DevOps, Python Full Stack with GenAI, MERN Full Stack, and Data Science tracks. These programs start from basic programming concepts and gradually move into advanced topics like frameworks, databases, cloud, and real-world application development

A major part of the training is hands-on project work, where students build multiple real-world applications like booking systems, e-commerce apps, and backend systems similar to industry platforms. The learning approach is based on “work-like training,” meaning students are expected to code regularly and work on guided as well as independent projects rather than just theory.

In addition to technical training, there is strong focus on DSA practice, interview preparation, and aptitude training, including mock interviews and coding assessments to prepare for placements. Some programs also claim internship-style exposure and continuous placement assistance with multiple interview opportunities

Overall, the training environment is structured, mentor-led, and placement-focused, with emphasis on practical skills, project building, and interview readiness rather than purely academic learning.
